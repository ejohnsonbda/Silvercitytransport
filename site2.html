The Editor's Note

Every ride matters.

Reliable rides. Professional service. Bermuda hospitality.

Silver City Transport is a Bermuda-based company providing dependable, convenient shuttle services for individuals, families, schools, businesses, visitors and groups of all sizes. Whether you are landing at the airport, planning a tour, organizing an event or arranging school transportation, we get you there safely, comfortably and on time.

Built on professionalism, safety and a customer-first approach — with clean, air-conditioned vehicles and friendly, knowledgeable drivers — we are more than a bus service. We are a mobility partner for the island, moving people with confidence, comfort and care, around the clock by booking.

Feature — 01

From the airport to island adventure.

The first impression of Bermuda should be an easy one. Our Silver Arrival service meets travelers at the airport and delivers them to hotel or home, with luggage assistance and a calm, on-time driver who knows the roads.

When the visit calls for more, the island opens up. A private tour traces the East End's forts and beaches, the harbor views of Hamilton, or the lighthouses and coves of the West End — at $130 per hour, routed entirely around you.

Departures are handled with the same care: a timely pickup, a smooth transfer, and a booking that can be reserved round-trip from the start.

Feature — 02

The art of the group journey.

Moving a group well is a quiet discipline. For businesses, our shuttles handle conferences, staff events and executive transfers on a schedule, with corporate billing and monthly invoicing arranged in advance.

For families and communities, the same vehicle carries church groups, sports teams, weddings, birthdays and reunions — everyone arriving together, no one left to find their own way.

And for schools and camps, we provide safe, scheduled runs with consistent drivers where possible, and clear communication with parents throughout.
