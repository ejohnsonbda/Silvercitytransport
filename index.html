About Silver City Transport

Bermuda's trusted partner in private & group transportation.

Reliable and convenient shuttle services for individuals, families, schools, businesses, visitors and groups of all sizes.

Built on professionalism, safety and dependable service, Silver City Transport offers airport runs, island tours, corporate transportation, school runs, beach runs, excursions, group outings and private shuttle services. Whether you need a comfortable ride from the airport, transportation for a special event, or organized travel for a group, we deliver a smooth and stress-free experience.

With 24/7 service availability, clean vehicles and a customer-first approach, we help people move around Bermuda with confidence, comfort and care.
